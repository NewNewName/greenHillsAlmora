Join us

Green Hills was registered as a Trust in Uttarakhand on June 2, 2014. Our organization invites all those who live and study or work in Almora District and are concerned about our environment to become members. The more like-minded people we can bring together, the stronger will be our voice. At present, there are about 800 young persons and 400 adults who have registered as members.
To join, all you need to do is make a commitment to take steps yourself to protect the environment and keep it clean and green as well as to encourage others to do so. A membership fee of a minimum of ₹ 2 for those under 18 years of age and ₹ 10 for adults should be paid to the treasurer, Mrs. Asha D’Souza, who will enter your name, mobile number, mohalla and e-mail address in the register of members.
